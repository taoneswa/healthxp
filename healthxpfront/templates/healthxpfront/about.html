Our Story

IPERI-ZIM (International Pharmacotherapy Education and Research Initiative) was founded in 2015 by a dedicated group of pharmacists and healthcare professionals who recognized the need for specialized education and research in pharmacotherapy in Zimbabwe.

Our organization was built on the belief that by enhancing the knowledge and skills of healthcare professionals, we can significantly improve patient care outcomes across the country.

Since our inception, we have trained hundreds of healthcare professionals, conducted numerous research projects, and established key partnerships with local and international healthcare organizations.

Our Mission

To enhance healthcare delivery in Zimbabwe through advanced pharmacotherapy education, innovative research, and professional development of healthcare practitioners.

Our Vision

To be the leading center of excellence in pharmacotherapy education and research in Southern Africa, contributing to improved patient outcomes and healthcare systems.
