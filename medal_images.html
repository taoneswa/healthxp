Instructions:

  1. Click each link to download the medal images
  2. Save them with the specified filenames
  3. Place them in the healthxpfront/static/healthxpfront/images/medals directory
  4. Make sure the images are in PNG format
  5. The recommended size is 150x150 pixels

Note: The suggested images are from Flaticon. You may need to create a free account to download them. Alternatively, you can use any other medal images of your choice, as long as you have the right to use them and maintain the same filenames.
